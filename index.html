clickplease.net challenges the unquestioned integration of advertisements within social media feeds. These advertisements are formatted and designed exactly the same as the content that we subscribe to; this strategy is profitable, but also raises questions about slippery slopes. How successfully should sponsored material visually mimic the content that we subscribe to? Should advertisements forced to have more obvious design elements that are visually different that the content we are subscribed to? How much authority do we give advertisers when it’s often unclear if the content is an advertisement in the first place? Where do we go from here?
